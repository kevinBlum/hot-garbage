# Hot Garbage — Quick Rules

**Goal:** End the game with the most valuable collection. Build **sets** (3+ of the same category) to multiply your score.

**Setup:** Everyone starts with §1000.

## On your turn (you're the Auctioneer)

1. You get an artifact. **Only you see what it's really worth.**
2. Everyone else sees its name and category — and your sales pitch.
3. Hype it. Lie if you want. The better the story, the higher the bids.
4. Players bid. Highest bid wins it and pays you.
5. If nobody bids, the Bank takes it for scraps — and you get almost nothing.

## When you're a bidder

- You only know the **category**, never the value.
- Bid on what you *need* for a set... but remember the seller knows you need it.
- Chaos can strike anytime: an **Appraiser** might reveal what something was really worth, and **Event cards** can flip the whole market.

## Scoring (revealed at the end)

- Own **3+ of one category** = that category's value is multiplied.
- **Forgeries** multiply the most (riskiest to collect). **Junk** the least.
- Leftover cash counts too — but a finished set usually beats a cash pile.

## The golden rule

The picture never lies about *what* a thing is — only about *what it's worth*. Read the room, not the receipt.
